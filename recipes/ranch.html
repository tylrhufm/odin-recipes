Homemade Ranch Dressing

Skip the store-bought stuff and make your own ranch at home! Our best ranch dressing recipe is incredibly easy to make with ingredients you likely already have in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ teaspoon dried chives
  • ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, sour cream, chives, parsley, dill, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until well-combined.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
